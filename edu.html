Education of prime Minister Sheikh Hasina

Sheikh Hasina took primary education in her home district Gopalgonj and moved to Dhaka eith her family in 1954. In 1956, she admitted to Nari Shiksha Mandir Girls' School at Tikatuli, Dhaka.Hasina Completed SSC Form Ajimpur Girls' School in 1965.In 1973, she received a graduate degree in Bangla from the University of Dhaka.It is a matter of shock , all of her family members were martyred on Agust 15, 1975 with a military coup except her and sister Sheikh Rehena.This was time, Hasina aling with her sister Rehena was Germany with Husband Wazed Miah.
